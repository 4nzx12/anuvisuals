Hi, I'm Anas Asif

Creative Director & Editor

I didn't start in a film school; I started on a smartphone screen in 2019 at the age of 12. What began as mobile animation experiments quickly evolved into a multi-disciplinary obsession with motion, editing, and visual storytelling. I don't just "edit videos"—I engineer them.

My workflow is a hybrid of creativity and technical precision. From freelancing and doing hobby projects in Dubai to crafting local brand identities in Thrissur, I've spent years refining a style that is distinctly modern and adaptable. I mainly specialize in short form, rhythmic, and visually appealing content.

I believe in being tool-agnostic, but my weapons of choice are : DaVinci Resolve for the Editing, VFX, ColorGrading ), Adobe Photoshop for Graphic Desgin, And Photo Correction. Adobe Lightroom For Still Photo Editing, Skin Correction, Batch Photo Editing ect.
