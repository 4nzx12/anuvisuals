Hi, I'm Anas Asif

Creative Director & Editor

I started on a smartphone in 2019 at the age of 12. No film school, just trial & error. What began as animation experiments evolved into a multi-disciplinary obsession. I don't just "edit videos". I engineer them.

My workflow is a hybrid of creative and technical precision. I specialize in short-form, rhythmic, and visually appealing content using DaVinci Resolve, Photoshop, and Lightroom.
