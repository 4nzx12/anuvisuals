Hi, I'm Anas Asif

Creative Director & Editor

I started on a smartphone in 2019 at the age of 12. No film school, No Courses, just trial & error. What began as animation experiments on mobile quickly evolved into a multi-disciplinary obsession with motion graphics, video editing, and visual storytelling. I don't just "edit videos". I engineer them.

My workflow is a hybrid of creative and technical precision. From freelancing and doing hobby projects in the UAE to crafting local brand identities in Thrissur & Malappuram, I've spent years refining a style that is distinctly modern and flashy. I mainly specialize in short form, rhythmic, and visually appealing content.

I believe in being tool-agnostic, and my weapons of choice are : DaVinci Resolve for the Editing, VFX, ColorGrading ), Adobe Photoshop for Graphic Desgin, And Photo Correction. Adobe Lightroom For Still Photo Editing, Skin Correction, Batch Photo Editing ect.
